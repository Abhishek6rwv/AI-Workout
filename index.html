    Workout Library

    Chest Workout

    Exercise: Push-ups

    Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

    Tips: Keep your back straight, engage your core, and lower chest to floor.

    Biceps Workout

    Exercise: Bicep Curls

    Reps: 3 sets of 10-15 reps

    Tips: Keep elbows close to your torso and move weights slowly.

    Shoulder Workout

    Exercise: Front Raises

    Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

    Tips: Avoid swinging arms; focus on controlled motion.

    Triceps Workout

    Exercise: Triceps Dips

    Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Tips: Keep your back close to the bench, elbows pointing backward.

    Back Workout

    Exercise: Bent Over Rows

    Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps

    Tips: Keep back flat, pull elbows to ribs, squeeze shoulder blades.

    Leg Workout

    Exercise: Squats

    Reps: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

    Tips: Keep chest up, knees behind toes, and lower slowly.

    Abs Workout

    Exercise: Crunches

    Reps: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

    Tips: Avoid pulling your neck; engage your core fully.

    Cardio

    Exercise: Jump Rope

    Duration: 15-20 minutes

    Tips: Land softly, keep core tight, maintain steady pace.

    Full Body Workout

    Exercise: Burpees

    Reps: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Tips: Land softly, move smoothly through each step, engage core.
